The Importance of a Clear Online Presence

A church website isn't just an address; it's a vital part of your ministry outreach. In today's digital age, your website often serves as the **first impression** for potential visitors, missionaries, and new members. Keep your sentences concise.

Keeping Your Message Simple

When writing content, imagine you are speaking directly to a new church visitor. Avoid complex jargon and ensure your service times, location, and beliefs are easily accessible.

"The goal is not to have a complex website, but a clear, welcoming digital doorstep for your community."

Checklist for Effective Content

  • **Service Times:** Must be the first thing a visitor sees.
  • **Contact Information:** Include an email and a map link.
  • **Statement of Faith:** Clearly state your FWB position.
  • **Mission Focus:** Highlight your local or international mission work.
